Multivariable Calculus Quiz

Question 1 (5 points)

Find the gradient of the function f(x,y) = x²y + sin(xy) at the point (π/2, 1).

Question 2 (5 points)

Compute the directional derivative of f(x,y) = 3x² - 2y² at the point (1,2) in the direction of the vector v = [3, 4]ᵀ.

Question 3 (5 points)

Find the critical points of f(x,y) = x³ + y³ - 3xy and classify them (local maximum, local minimum, or saddle point).

Question 4 (5 points)

Use Lagrange multipliers to find the maximum and minimum values of f(x,y) = xy subject to the constraint x² + y² = 8.

Instructions: Show all your work. Partial credit will be given for correct setup even if the final answer is incorrect. Circle your final answers.
