Genetics and Molecular Biology Midterm Exam

Question 1 (25 points)

Explain the difference between DNA replication and transcription. Include in your answer:

  • The purpose of each process
  • The enzymes involved
  • The products generated
Question 2 (25 points)

In pea plants, tall (T) is dominant to short (t) and yellow seeds (Y) are dominant to green seeds (y).

a) If a heterozygous tall, heterozygous yellow plant is crossed with a short, green plant, what is the probability of getting a short plant with yellow seeds?

b) What percentage of the offspring will be homozygous recessive for both traits?

Question 3 (25 points)

Describe the process of protein synthesis from DNA to functional protein. Be sure to include:

  • Transcription (including initiation, elongation, and termination)
  • RNA processing
  • Translation (including the roles of mRNA, tRNA, and ribosomes)
Question 4 (25 points)

A DNA sequence is: 5'-ATGCGCTA-3'

a) What is the complementary DNA strand?

b) What is the mRNA transcript that would be synthesized from the template strand?

c) What is the amino acid sequence that would be translated from this mRNA? (Use the genetic code table provided on the last page)

Bonus Question (10 points)

Explain how mutations can lead to genetic variation and discuss one mechanism by which cells repair DNA damage.
